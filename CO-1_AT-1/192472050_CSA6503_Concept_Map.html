Concept Map

CSA6503 – Concept Map

Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI & Large Language Models

🤖
Layer 1 · Broadest Field

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The broad field of creating systems capable of performing tasks requiring human intelligence.

Example Self-driving cars (Tesla)
AI → ML: Machine Learning is a subset of Artificial Intelligence.
📊
Layer 2 · Subset of AI

Machine Learning (ML)

Algorithms that learn patterns from data without being explicitly programmed.

Example Netflix Recommendation System
ML → DL: Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning that uses multi-layer neural networks.
🧠
Layer 3 · Subset of ML

Deep Learning (DL)

A branch of Machine Learning using neural networks with many hidden layers.

Example Face Recognition (Apple Face ID)
DL → Generative AI: Generative AI uses deep learning models to generate new content.
🎨
Layer 4 · Applies DL

Generative AI

AI capable of generating new text, images, music, code, and other content.

Example Adobe Firefly
Generative AI → LLMs: Large Language Models are a type of Generative AI specialized for understanding and generating human language.
💬
Layer 5 · Specialized Generative AI

Large Language Models (LLMs)

Large neural networks trained on massive text datasets to understand and generate human language.

Example ChatGPT
